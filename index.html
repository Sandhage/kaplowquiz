Instructions

  • Press the GO! button to start the timer.
  • Hints will appear briefly giving you all of the correct answers to defuse the bomb!
  • After the hints disappear, you will have 24 seconds to correctly answer all of the questions or... KAPLOW! You lose!
  • For most of the questions, you just need to click on the correct answer; in the case of a text-box, just type the answer and press enter!
